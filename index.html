Biography

Fatemeh Shiri is a Research Fellow, working with A/Prof. Yuan-Fang Li and Prof. Reza Haffari in the Vision and Language discipline group, Faculty of Information Technology, Monash University. Fatemeh was a Sessional Lecturer and Research Fellow at Deakin University before commencing at Monash. She obtained her PhD in Computer Science from the Australian National University (ANU) and Data61-CSIRO in 2020, under the supervision of Prof. Richard Hartley and Prof. Fatih Porikli and Dr Piotr Konisuz. Her research interests lie at the intersection of natural language processing, computer vision, and deep learning. Her current research focuses on efficient NLP methods to make Generative AI models more accessible to multimodal information extraction and knowledge base construction.
